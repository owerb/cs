3. Some of the Robotic types are:

- Industrial Robots: An industrial robot is a robot system used for manufacturing. Industrial robots are automated,
programmable and capable of movement.
- Military Robots: Most military robots are teleoperated and do not carry weapons; their functions include disarming explosive devices, sniper detection, reconnaissance, and surveillance.
The robots that are now outfitted with weapons are controlled remotely,
meaning they are unable to carry out independent killing.
- Medical Robots: A medical robot is a robot used in the medical sciences.
They include surgical robots.
These are in most tele manipulators, which use the surgeon's activators on one side
to control the effector on the other side.
